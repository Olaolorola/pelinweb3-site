PelinWeb3 exists to onboard Nigerian gamers, learners, and Unity developers into the Web3 ecosystem through QUDO-powered games and Bitdegree-led learning—empowering a new digital generation.

From Q3 2025 to Q2 2026, we launch campaigns, quests, bootcamps, and onboarding in Nigeria—then expand to Ghana and Kenya.
